‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I activate voice commands?

Voice commands are enabled by default. Simply say "Hey ASOOS" followed by your command, or tap the microphone icon. Supported commands include:

  • "diamond status" - Check Diamond CLI status
  • "deploy app" - Deploy applications
  • "check wings" - View wing formations
  • "victory status" - Check Victory36 protection
Q: What is Dream Commander?

Dream Commander is our AI decision engine that processes up to 11 million decisions per day. It provides predictive infrastructure management, wing formation optimization, and intelligent system recommendations to keep your infrastructure running smoothly.

Q: How secure is my biometric data?

Your biometric data (TouchID, FaceID, fingerprints) never leaves your device. We use Apple's Secure Enclave and Android's hardware security module to store biometric information locally. Additionally, all data is protected by our Victory36 security framework.

Q: Can I use ASOOS Mobile offline?

Yes! ASOOS Mobile includes offline sync capabilities. Commands are queued when disconnected and automatically executed when connectivity is restored. Critical monitoring data is cached locally for offline viewing.

üîß Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

Voice commands not working:

  • Check microphone permissions in Settings
  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Try restarting the app

Biometric authentication failing:

  • Re-enroll fingerprint/face in device settings
  • Clean camera lens or fingerprint sensor
  • Use backup PIN authentication

Diamond CLI connection issues:

  • Verify your credentials in SallyPort
  • Check firewall settings
  • Contact your IT administrator

App crashes or freezes:

  • Force-close and restart the app
  • Update to the latest version
  • Restart your device
  • Contact support with crash details
