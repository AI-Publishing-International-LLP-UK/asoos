Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 6, 2025
Last Updated: October 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Publishing International LLP ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the ASOOS Mobile applications (iOS and Android) and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account registration information (email, name)
  • Voice commands and interactions with our AI assistants
  • Infrastructure configuration data (encrypted)
  • Support communications and feedback

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (iOS/Android version, device model)
  • App usage analytics and performance metrics
  • Location data (only if explicitly permitted for enterprise features)
  • Biometric authentication data (stored locally on device only)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve ASOOS Mobile services
  • Process voice commands through our Dream Commander system
  • Authenticate access to your infrastructure systems
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Send important service updates and security notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI voice assistants

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who assist in providing our services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

4. Data Security and Protection

We implement enterprise-grade security measures:

  • Victory36 Protection Framework: Our proprietary security system
  • End-to-End Encryption: All infrastructure data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Biometric Security: Authentication data remains on your device
  • SallyPort Authentication: Multi-layer security verification
  • Regular Security Audits: Continuous monitoring and improvement

5. Voice Data and AI Processing

Our AI voice assistants (including Dr. Lucy, Dr. Claude, and 12 other specialized assistants) process voice commands to provide infrastructure management capabilities:

  • Voice data is processed using ElevenLabs and Hume AI technologies
  • Commands are analyzed by our Dream Commander decision engine (11M decisions/day)
  • Voice recordings are not permanently stored after command processing
  • You can disable voice features at any time in app settings

6. International Data Transfers

Our services operate globally with data centers in:

  • United States (us-west1, us-central1)
  • Europe (eu-west1)

We ensure appropriate safeguards for international data transfers in compliance with GDPR and other applicable regulations.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Request data portability or restriction of processing
  • Disable voice command features
  • Delete your account and associated data

8. Children's Privacy

ASOOS Mobile is designed for enterprise and professional use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of ASOOS Mobile after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Third-Party Services

ASOOS Mobile integrates with various third-party services:

  • Google Cloud Platform: Infrastructure hosting and secrets management
  • Cloudflare: Content delivery and security
  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Firebase: Analytics and crash reporting
  • ElevenLabs & Hume AI: Voice processing and synthesis

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.
