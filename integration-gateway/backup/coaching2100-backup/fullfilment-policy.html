Coaching 2100 World-Class Quality and Guarantee Policy

At Coaching 2100, you deserve nothing less than the best. We are committed to ensuring that our products and services exceed your expectations. That’s why we’ve designed generous, transparent, and world-class policies to ensure your complete satisfaction at every step.

Refund Policy

We stand by the quality of our services and aim to give you peace of mind with our extended refund guarantees. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we will work tirelessly to make things right.

How to Get a Refund

Digital Products & Online Services:

  • A 45-day satisfaction guarantee applies to all digital products.
  • Refunds must be requested within 45 days of purchase.
  • If more than 25% of a course is accessed, partial refunds are available on a prorated basis.
  • Transaction fees are fully refundable.

Coaching Sessions:

  • If you are not satisfied after your first session, you may request a full refund before the second session.
  • Unused sessions that are prepaid are fully refundable, with no time limit.

First-Time Buyer Guarantee

For first-time Coaching 2100 clients, we offer a No-Risk Satisfaction Guarantee. If you’re unsatisfied within the first 14 days of your purchase, you can request a full refund, no questions asked.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email support@coaching2100.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and provide:

  • Your full name
  • Order number
  • Date of purchase
  • Product or service purchased
  • Reason for requesting the refund (optional for feedback purposes)

Our team will respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds will be credited back to the original method of payment within 7 business days.

Satisfaction Guaranteed

We are committed to working with you until you're satisfied.

Customized Coaching Programs:

We will collaborate with you on customized coaching programs until your goals are met. If, after completing the full program, you're still unsatisfied, we will offer a partial refund or additional sessions at no extra charge.

Group Coaching Sessions & Workshops:

You may request a 100% credit toward future services if you’re not satisfied after the first session. If you cannot attend a group session, you can transfer your spot to another participant at no cost.

Non-Refundable Items (Clear and Transparent)

Certain items are non-refundable due to their nature, but we’ll always inform you of this before purchase. If we fail to notify you, a full refund will be issued. Non-refundable items include:

  • Customized coaching programs once more than 50% is completed.
  • Group coaching sessions or workshops after the first session.
  • Digital products once more than 50% of the content has been accessed.
  • Promotional or discounted offers, unless stated otherwise.

Delivery Policy (Real-Time Transparency)

We ensure timely delivery and seamless access to all services.

Digital Products:

Upon payment, digital products such as eBooks, guides, and templates are delivered instantly via email or through our online platform. Real-time tracking will be provided.

Online Courses:

Access to online courses is provided within 24 hours of payment. You will receive login credentials via email.

Coaching Sessions:

After booking, you will receive confirmation with session details within 24 hours, and a reminder 48 hours before your session.

Real-Time Service Tracking:

For phased or ongoing services like mentorship programs, clients will have access to real-time progress updates, ensuring full transparency on delivery timelines.

Flexible Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change, so we offer flexible and fair cancellation terms to protect your time and investment.

Coaching Sessions:

You may cancel or reschedule any coaching session free of charge up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellations made within 24 hours will incur no more than a 25% fee.

Online Courses:

You may transfer your course access to another participant at no cost if you cannot continue. If less than 50% of the course is accessed, a 50% refund is available.

Subscription Cancellations:

You can cancel ongoing subscriptions at any time. The cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle, with no further charges. Access to services will remain until the end of the billing period.
